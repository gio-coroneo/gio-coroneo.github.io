Not clean enough:

( Summary )I designed an information design installation that collects comments from people who don't fit the "clean girl" aesthetic, to show how a seemingly neutral trend is actually normative and creates frustration, inadequacy, and exclusion.

( Scope )( Data Design, Brand Identity, Motion Design, UX Design )

( About the Installation )The analysis focuses on comments from users who say
they cannot conform to the "clean girl" aesthetic, naming what feels impossible.
The installation uses this to expose the normative nature of a seemingly neutral trend.

Posing as natural, the aesthetic in fact breeds frustration, inadequacy, and exclusion. These testimonies let us identify and connect the main reasons for exclusion, revealing the mechanisms through which the trend generates insecurity.

( Exhibition layout )The installation is divided into two main areas:

1. Exhibition area – the installation itself, dedicated to displaying the comments.
2. Evaluation area – a space to collect feedback on what viewers actually grasped.

( Introduction )The installation opens with a video in four sections, one for each of the four macro-categories that emerged from the analysis. Each is embodied by an AI-generated model: in every video gestures and objects convey the spirit of the four issues.

( Audience Experience )By interacting directly with the visualization, visitors gradually engage — at different levels — with the comments, and thus with the "gaps" expressed by those who do not see themselves reflected in the main trend.

The audience gradually comes to understand that these gaps fall into a number of broad macro-categories, and observes each one individually, one at a time. Next, they slowly approach the Evaluation Area.

Visitors carefully follow the step-by-step instructions printed above the console and apply to a set of "raw," uncategorized comments the same categorization logic that was originally used in designing the visualization.

The goal is to check whether they have understood the underlying logic.
