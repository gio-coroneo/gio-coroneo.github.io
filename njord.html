Giovanni Coroneo

NJÖRD:
Shape your Desire.

( Summary )A speculative design project that pushes the buying and eating salmon into a near future where colour becomes
a consumer choice. It starts from salmoFan, a Pantone swatch that fixes the colour of farmed salmon, and imagines a service
that lets anyone tint their fish to taste.

( Scope )( 3D Design, Brand Identity,
Speculative Design, UX Design )

( The Real Practice )The flesh of farmed salmon is naturally pale, close to white. Because almost no one would buy a colourless fillet, producers add pigment to
the feed and pick the final shade from a colour fan.

The colour of the salmon we eat is, quite literally, a decision made from a swatch. NJÖRD begins exactly here, taking a practice that already exists and following
it to its logical conclusion.

( SalmoFan )At the centre of the system is salmoFan, a swatch fan that codifies the pigmentation of salmon the way a Pantone book codifies ink. Each customer can browse the different shades and create a fillet to suit their own tastes.

( The Speculation )If the colour is already chosen for us, why not hand the choice to the buyer? NJÖRD imagines a retail service for custom salmon, where the customer — not the producer — decides how the fish should look before it reaches the plate.

The scenario is deliberately frictionless and ordinary, so that the absurdity reads as a natural extension of how we already shop. Speculative design uses plausible futures to question the present we now accept.

( The Console )The service is held together by a communication machine: a console that stages the act of customisation. It turns an unsettling idea into a smooth, retail-grade interaction, where colouring a living thing feels no different from picking a paint sample.

( A Critique )Behind the product sits a critique of the human habit of reshaping nature on a whim. By making the manipulation explicit, desirable, and even fun, NJÖRD asks how far convenience can go before a preference becomes a quiet form of violence.

The salmon is no longer a fish. It is a colour you can order.
